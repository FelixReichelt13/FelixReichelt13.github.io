I am a PhD candidate in Genetic Epidemiology at the University Medical Center Groningen (UMCG). My research examines gene–environment interplay in stress exposure and stress response in relation to cardiometabolic health. Ongoing work includes pedigree-based heritability and genetic correlation analyses in the Lifelines Cohort, focusing on stress exposures (childhood trauma, long-term difficulties, stressful life events, social support, loneliness) and Big Five traits (neuroticism, extraversion, conscientiousness).
